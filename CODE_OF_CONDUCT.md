# OpenInfra User Group Kenya - Code of Conduct

The OpenInfra User Group Kenya is part of the global OpenInfra Foundation community.
Our goal is to create a space where individuals and organizations can **learn, collaborate, and innovate** around open infrastructure technologies through meetups, mentorship, and proof-of-concepts (PoCs).

We follow the **OpenInfra Foundation Code of Conduct**, adapting it to reflect our local context and community safety standards.

# Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free, inclusive, and respectful experience for everyone, regardless of age, gender identity, sexual orientation, disability, physical appearance, ethnicity, religion, or technical experience level.

We commit to promoting:

 - Respectful communication and behavior
 - Constructive collaboration
 - Diversity of thought and background
 - Privacy and safety in all community interactions
